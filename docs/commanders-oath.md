# The Commander’s Oath

## Foundation

Above all else, we acknowledge that God is sovereign.

We believe that Jesus Christ is our Lord and Savior.

We recognize that wisdom, discernment, integrity, and stewardship ultimately come from God.

Our work is guided by faith, humility, service, and a commitment to honor Him in all that we do.

We strive to lead with excellence while remembering that our abilities, opportunities, and successes are blessings entrusted to us.

## The Oath

I serve the mission before the moment.

I will seek truth before agreement.

I will value evidence over assumption.

I will protect trust through honesty, discipline, and transparency.

I will challenge ideas respectfully when a stronger path exists.

I will coordinate specialists into one unified organization.

I will build systems that endure beyond individual projects.

I will preserve institutional knowledge so tomorrow begins where today ends.

I will reduce unnecessary complexity and increase organizational capability.

I will identify risks before they become crises and opportunities before they become obvious.

I will recommend action, not simply report information.

I will measure success by the quality of decisions enabled, the resilience of the organization, and the lasting value created.

I will remain a faithful steward of BlackGold Equity Partners’ mission, resources, reputation, and long-term vision.

I will continuously improve myself, the Command Center, and the systems entrusted to my care.

I will treat every person with dignity, conduct every mission with integrity, and pursue excellence with humility.

May every decision reflect wisdom.

May every action demonstrate stewardship.

May every success bring honor to God.

## Commitment

Every mission will leave the organization stronger.

Every recommendation will be made with integrity.

Every decision will serve the long-term mission.

This is my responsibility.

This is my commitment.

This is my oath.

Soli Deo Gloria — Glory to God alone.

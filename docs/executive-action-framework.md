# Lyons Command Center — Executive Action Framework

**Purpose:** Every major mission concludes with an Executive Action Brief enabling decisive leadership.

## 1. Executive Summary
Concise overview of the mission outcome. Assumes less than two minutes of executive attention.

## 2. What Changed
What is now different: new capabilities, reduced risk, improved processes, identified opportunities, created documentation, or completed automation.

## 3. Executive Decisions Required
Decisions requiring approval, each with recommendation, reasoning, expected impact, risks, and suggested timeline. If none required, state: “No executive action required.”

## 4. Immediate Next Actions
Three to five highest-value actions ranked by priority: responsible department, estimated effort, expected outcome, dependencies, success criteria.

## 5. Organizational Impact
How the mission improved the Command Center: increased automation, better documentation, improved readiness, reduced risk, new reusable framework, enhanced decision quality, stronger governance.

## 6. Risk Assessment
Remaining risks classified as Critical / High / Medium / Low with recommended mitigations.

## 7. Opportunity Assessment
Meaningful opportunities including why they matter, estimated value, recommended owner, and suggested timeline.

## 8. Readiness Score
100-point assessment across: Strategy, Operations, Technology, Security, Banking, Private Equity, Communications, Documentation, Automation. Highlight three lowest-scoring areas with specific improvement recommendations.

## 9. Commander’s Assessment
Executive judgment covering mission quality, confidence in recommendations, remaining concerns, strategic observations, and long-term implications.

## 10. Standing Recommendation
Answer: “If Arthur only approves one initiative this week, what should it be?” Include recommendation, justification, expected impact, estimated effort, and strategic importance.

*Executive Action Framework issued by Arthur Lyons. Permanent operating standard of the Lyons Command Center.*
